The Mkurugenzi

From radio host to Kenya's most influential storyteller, Abel Mutua's journey is a testament to the power of authentic narrative.

My Story & Vision

I am Abel Mutua, a Kenyan filmmaker, scriptwriter, director, and actor known for my work in productions like "Real Househelps of Kawangware," "Stories of Our Lives," and my popular YouTube channel.

My journey began in radio before transitioning to television writing and eventually filmmaking. I believe in telling authentic African stories that reflect our society, culture, and everyday experiences.

As "Mkurugenzi" (The Director), I'm committed to nurturing new talent and creating content that not only entertains but also educates and inspires meaningful conversations.

The Complete Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Kenya's most influential storyteller

The Complete Journey

My story begins in the bustling streets of Nairobi, where I discovered my passion for storytelling through everyday interactions and observations of Kenyan life.

Radio became my first canvas, where I learned to paint pictures with words and create characters that resonated with audiences across the airwaves.

The transition to television and film was natural, but it required dedication and a deep understanding of visual storytelling that I cultivated through years of practice.

Educational Background

Formal education at the Kenya Institute of Mass Communication provided the technical foundation, but real-world experience in radio stations and production houses shaped my practical skills.

Mentorship & Teaching

Today, I'm committed to mentoring the next generation of African storytellers through workshops, masterclasses, and personal guidance sessions.
